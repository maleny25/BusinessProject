Interact Club

Who we are

Interact Club is a community service club. We are a group of High Schoolers who are passionate about helping others. Our club advisor is Lori Lowensen who is very commited to our club and helping others. We do community service in our local community of Castroville. We are active all year and work on multiple things. We raise our funds and are thankful for those who donatae to us.

What we do

As an Interact Club, we work on a variety of projects. We have created a family resource center for the low income families in our community. We feed the homeless when we make PB & J's.
